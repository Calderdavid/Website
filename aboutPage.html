Our practice

In the knowledge and image-intensive world that we live in, clients are well-
informed and generally know what they want, they just need some help to
articulate it.

R Architecture has passion for bringing great
design to suburban contexts.

We work hard to understand our clients' requirements and we share their
aspirations. We build strong, trusting relationships as we guide them through
the design and construction process.

Our project work is diverse. However, every project delivers on our clients'
dreams and makes an exemplary contribution to the community.
